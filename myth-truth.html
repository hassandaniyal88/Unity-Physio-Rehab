Physiotherapy: Myths vs Truth

Myth: Physiotherapy is only for elderly people.

Truth: Physiotherapy benefits individuals of all ages — from athletes and office workers to children, post-surgery patients, and seniors.

Modern physiotherapy is tailored to your needs — whether you're recovering from a sports injury, managing chronic pain, or improving posture and mobility.

Another common myth is that physiotherapy is just exercise. In reality, it includes manual therapy, electrotherapy, dry needling, education, and guided rehabilitation plans customized to your condition.
