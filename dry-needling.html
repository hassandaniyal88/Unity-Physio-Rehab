What is Dry Needling?

Dry needling is a modern treatment technique designed to ease muscular pain. It involves inserting fine needles into trigger points (knots) in your muscles to reduce tension, improve mobility, and promote healing.

This therapy is based on Western medicine principles and anatomy. The term "dry" distinguishes it from acupuncture, as it doesnâ€™t involve injecting any fluid into the body.

Dry needling can help reduce muscle tightness, improve blood flow, and relieve pain caused by:

  • Sports injuries
  • Muscle spasms
  • Back or neck pain
  • Postural issues
  • Chronic pain conditions

Sessions are often short and can be combined with other physiotherapy methods for enhanced results. It is a safe and effective technique when performed by trained professionals.
